Front-End Developer in Portugal

My passion for technology and web development ignited when I discovered the exciting world of programming through Harvard's CS50 course. This initial spark led me to dive deeper into web development through Udemy's 'Web Developer Bootcamp,' where I mastered HTML and CSS fundamentals, and their 'CSS - MASTERING ANIMATIONS' course, which taught me how to bring websites to life with engaging animations. This new chapter has ignited a fire in me - I'm now determined to transition into front-end development, combining my creative energy and problem-solving skills to build beautiful, user-friendly websites.

As an adventurous spirit and passionate wakeboarder, I've always believed in chasing dreams and embracing new challenges. My journey in the water sports world has led me to my current role as a social host at Hip-notics cable park in Turkey, where I create unforgettable experiences for guests from around the world. Before this, my professional path took me from working as an Animal Production Engineer, where I led teams and optimized livestock systems, to teaching young football players and sharing my love for wakeboarding as a Vice-President of the Portugal Wake Association.

My experience in leadership roles, coupled with my natural enthusiasm for learning and creating, makes me excited about this new challenge. I'm ready to dive into the tech world with the same dedication that helped me succeed in animal production and sports. The thrill of creating something from scratch, much like the rush of landing a new wakeboard trick, drives me forward in this exciting career change!
